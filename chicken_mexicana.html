Overview

Chicken Mexicana combines seasoned chicken pieces with colourful peppers and onions for a zesty, satisfying bite. It's commonly offered in Domino's Nigeria menus as a popular choice for those who like a peppery profile.

Toppings (typical)

  • Seasoned chicken
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomato sauce & cheese
